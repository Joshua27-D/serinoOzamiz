Welcome to Ozamiz

Ozamiz is a city in Misamis Occidental, Philippines, known for its history and natural beauty. It is by the peaceful Panguil Bay, with great views and fresh air. The city has the Cotta Shrine, an old fort that shows its past and is important to many people. You can also visit Hoyohoy Highland Park, a place with beautiful views and fun activities. Ozamiz is a quiet and friendly city, perfect for visitors and locals.

Mission

To foster a progressive, inclusive, and sustainable community that celebrates cultural heritage, preserves natural resources, and ensures the well-being of all citizens through responsive governance, economic development, and active participation.
